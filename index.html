Our Mission

We believe that every kitty deserves a loving home. We want to create an environment where cats can find their person, and people can have an awesome cup of coffee. To that end, several of the items on our menu are named after our resident furry friends!

Biscuit's Bros

Biscuit

Our owner's cat, Biscuit loves to pounce around the cafe. He's a rowdy little guy, but always ready for love!

Breakfast Sandwich

Breakfast Sandwich is the newest addition to our crew, found chewing on some of our coffee beans. He's a little skittish, but once he's warmed up to you, he'll never leave your side!

Eggs McGee

Eggs McGee is one of our elderly cats, always ready for a nap. If you're lucky, you might find him snoozing right next to you- or on your lap!

Chonky Boy

Chonky Boy is one of our more adventurous kitties- always on the hunt for fun!

Qishta

Qishta is a refined lady who loves to greet visitors to Biscuit's Biscuits and Coffee Co. You'll typically find her near the entrance- but if she follows you, she must like you a lot!

Luna

Luna is always excited to see guests, and loves to play! You'll often see her playing with the toys around the cafe, and she'd love for you to join her!

Jerry

Jerry is a bit of an exciteable fellow, so he mostly resides in the rafters of the cafe. Every once in a while, though, he'll find a person he likes and stick with them for their stay!

Bubbles

Bubbles is a bit of an airhead, but she's got Harold to help her out! The two are inseparable.

Harold

Surprisingly for an orange cat, Harold has a good head on her shoulders. She's inseparable from her best bud Bubbles!

Whipped Cream

Whipped Cream is as sweet as her name implies, and loves to get pet! She'll play with you all day long.

Emily Katchadourian

Nathan Perry

Chad Sanchez
